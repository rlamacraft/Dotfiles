* Charging sound
- Play the iOS charging sound when the power cord is connected
#+BEGIN_SRC sh
defaults write com.apple.PowerChime ChimeOnAllHardware -bool true; open /System/Library/CoreServices/PowerChime.app &
#+END_SRC
